Nifty App

Spring 2016
Challenge

Client requested conception and design of Nifty: An app to allow women to save money by renting dresses from others around them.

Role

UI
UX
Branding

Solution

I worked with another designer to create Nifty an app that went from concept to a fully designed prototype. Users can rent dresses out to people around them, or browse for dresses they might want to rent. Users have the option to message the renter or directly rent the dress by following easy to understand prompts.
