Forbacon.co

Web Design, UX, UI, Branding | January - April 2016
The Challenge

Food blog needed to establish a brand and redesign the current site.

The Process

After adjusting the current logo, I started my process with an art direction phase. Here, I worked with the client to create a new color scheme, new photography direction, and get a feel for certain UI elements that she liked. Once the general aesthetic was established, I began the wire framing stage. I made notable improvements including adding the option to filter your recipe search. Once the wireframes were complete, I added what I learned from art direction to create high fidelity mock ups. I started with desktop first, keeping mobile in mind.

The Solution

I worked along side the client to create a new brand for this food blog, including retouching the current logo, creating custom iconography and choosing a photography style and color scheme. I also completely redesigned current site with desktop and responsive views.
