Coffee Bar

Branding, Packaging, Print | August - September 2016
The Challenge

New Grounds Food was seeking an extension of their product, CoffeeBar, to an additional 2 flavors. Also, they required a complete redesign of their secondary box packaging.

The Solution

I worked with the founders of this student venture to collect assets, including ingredient photography and new colors, to assemble the two new flavors of the CoffeeBar. The secondary box packaging included working with the manufacturer to adjust sizes as the process went along. I created a completely new box for the these bars to live in. In addition to the packaging redesign, I created a style guide for CoffeeBar (not pictured to preserve privacy). I developed content and executed in the creation of the style guide which will provide guidance for others who wish to work on their brand. www.newgroundsfood.com
